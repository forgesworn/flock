⬇  Download flock for Android

Free. Works on any Android 7+ phone, including GrapheneOS. No Google Play, no Google account, nothing phones home.

Install it (takes a minute)

  1. Open the downloaded file. Pull down your notifications and tap it.
  2. If your phone asks, allow your browser to install unknown apps. That is Android's normal question for any app that skips the app store. Why: flock is deliberately not in the Play Store, so no Google account or tracking is involved in getting it.
  3. When you first share in the app, choose location “Allow all the time”. Android takes you to Settings for this. Why: this lets flock notice you leaving a safe place while the phone is in your pocket. It does not mean your location is shared all the time. Sharing still follows flock's rules: only on a breach, a pick-up, or a call for help.
  4. Allow the notification when asked. Why: Android requires a visible notice while an app watches location in the background. It only appears while sharing is on.
  5. GrapheneOS only: set Settings → Apps → flock → Battery → Unrestricted. Why: otherwise the system may quietly stop the watch to save power.

Updates: download the new file and install it over the top. Your circles and settings stay.

Verify your download (optional)

Compare the file's fingerprint with flock.apk.sha256:

sha256sum flock.apk

The two long strings should match exactly.

Why the app instead of the website?

The app can keep watch in the background. It notices you leaving a safe place even with the screen off. The website only works while it is open.

There is no account and no sign-up. flock makes an anonymous key that lives only on your phone, so nothing links it to you. Use it as a guest and reset it whenever.

Any phone, no install

Open flock.forgesworn.dev and add it to your home screen (iPhone: Share → Add to Home Screen). Everything works while the app is open, including SOS, pick-ups and the live map.
