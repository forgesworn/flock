Download flock for Android

Works on any Android 7+ phone, including GrapheneOS — no Google Play services needed.

  1. Open the downloaded file (pull down your notifications and tap it).
  2. If your phone asks, allow your browser to install unknown apps — that's Android's normal question for any app that doesn't come from an app store. Why: flock is deliberately not in the Play Store — no Google account or tracking involved in getting it.
  3. When you first share in the app, choose location “Allow all the time” (Android takes you to Settings for this). Why: that's what lets flock notice a safe-place breach while your phone is in your pocket. It does not mean your location is shared all the time — sharing still follows flock's rules: only on a breach, a pick-up, or a call for help.
  4. Allow the notification when asked. Why: Android requires a visible notice while an app watches location in the background. It only appears while sharing is on.
  5. GrapheneOS only: set Settings → Apps → flock → Battery → Unrestricted. Why: otherwise the system may quietly stop the watch to save power.

Updates: download the new file and install it over the top — your circles and settings stay.

Verify your download (optional)

Compare the file's fingerprint with flock.apk.sha256:

sha256sum flock.apk

The two long strings should match exactly.
