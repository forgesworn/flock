Report misuse

Use this route for suspected illegal use, stalking, coercive control, a child-safety concern, a privacy violation, or another breach of the flock Terms.

Last updated 10 July 2026

This is not an emergency service. If anyone is in immediate danger, contact the police or emergency services first. In the UK call 999 or 112. Elsewhere, use the local emergency number.

Send a report

Email abuse@forgesworn.dev. You may report on your own behalf or for another person. State whether anyone is in immediate danger and whether a child may be involved.

Include only what is reasonably needed:

  • what happened and why you believe it involves flock;
  • the approximate dates and times;
  • whether the hosted service, an operator-provided relay, or a self-hosted deployment was used;
  • any public event identifier or public key that is safe to share; and
  • how we can contact you, unless you choose not to identify yourself.

Do not send a private key, backup code, circle seed, invitation secret, PIN, password, or someone's exact current location. Redact screenshots and documents where possible.

What the operator can do

flock's operator cannot normally read encrypted messages or locations and cannot recover data held only on a device. A report may therefore be needed to identify the affected operator-controlled service. We may preserve the minimum relevant records, restrict access to infrastructure we control, address a security defect, ask for proportionate clarification, or refer a credible threat or legal requirement to the appropriate authority.

We cannot remove data from a recipient's device or guarantee deletion by an independent relay or self-hosted operator.

Privacy and fair handling

Report information is used only to assess and respond to the concern, protect people and the Services, meet legal duties, and establish or defend legal claims. It is restricted to people who need it and retained only as long as reasonably necessary for those purposes. See the Privacy Policy.

Complaints and review

If you believe a report or restriction was handled incorrectly, ask for a review at legal@forgesworn.dev. Privacy rights requests should go to privacy@forgesworn.dev.

Misuse and safeguarding reports: abuse@forgesworn.dev.
